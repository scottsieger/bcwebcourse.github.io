Scott Sieger is a current sophomore student in the Morrissey College of Arts and Sciences at Boston College. He is pursuing a degree in Economics along with minors in Computer Science and Finance. He hopes to find a career in the financial technology field in the near future. Scott grew up on Long Island, New York and attended Mepham High School. He ran track and cross country all four years of high school and was the captain for both teams his senior year. Scott graduated in 2018 as an AP Scholar with honor.
