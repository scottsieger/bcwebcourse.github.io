Psychological Disorders: What We Know

Scott Sieger
Psychology as a Natural Science, Professor Gene Heyman, 2019

There are a multitude of psychological disorders affecting the brain identified by an even greater amount of symptoms and signs. Due to extensive experiments and biological as well as hereditary research, the scientific community has learned a lot about certain brain disorders, and how to treat them. The disorders discussed in the following paper are:

  • Chronic Traumatic Encephalopathy (CTE)
  • Narcolepsy
  • Tourettes
  • Schizophrenia
  • Huntington's Disease
